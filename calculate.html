How to use?

Using the GPA Calculator:
1. Add Courses:

Select the grade you obtained for each course from the dropdown menu.

Enter the credit hours associated with each course.

Click the "Add Courses" button to add the course to the list.


2. Calculate GPA:

Once you've added all your courses, click the "Calculate" button to compute your GPA.

Your GPA will be displayed below the button.


3. Clear Entries:

To start over or clear your entries, click the "Clear Courses" button.

This will reset the GPA calculator for new calculations.

Using the CGPA Calculator:
1. Add Semesters:

Enter the GPA achieved for each semester in the input field provided.

Click the "Add Semesters" button to add the semester to the list.


2. Calculate GPA:

Once you've added all your semesters, click the "Calculate" button to compute your CGPA.

Your CGPA will be displayed below the button.


3. Clear Entries:

To clear your semester entries, click the "Clear Semesters" button.

This will reset the CGPA calculator for new calculations..

Tips for Accuracy:

Ensure you enter the correct grades and credit hours for each course/semester.

Review your entries before calculating to avoid errors.

Use the tool regularly to keep track of your academic progress.

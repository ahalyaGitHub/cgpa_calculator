Welcome to Our CGPA Calculator

Our CGPA calculator is a handy tool designed to help students effortlessly calculate their Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA). Whether you're a high school student, college student, or university student, our calculator simplifies the process of determining your academic performance.



How It Works:

Input Your Grades:

Enter the grades you've received for each of your courses. Our calculator supports a variety of grading scales, making it suitable for students worldwide.


Enter Credit Hours:

Assign credit hours to each course. This allows our calculator to weigh courses based on their credit value.


Calculate Your CGPA:

With the click of a button, our calculator computes your CGPA based on the grades and credit hours you've entered.
